    Quick wellness tips

    • 5-4-3-2-1 grounding: Name 5 things you see, 4 you hear, 3 you can touch, 2 you smell, 1 you taste.
    • Box breathing: Inhale 4s → hold 4s → exhale 4s → hold 4s. Repeat 4 times.
    • Study breaks: Use the Pomodoro method — 25 min study, 5 min break.
    • Sleep hygiene: Try to sleep and wake at the same time daily, even during exams.
    • Talk it out: Share your worries with a friend, family member, or counsellor.
